Data Description

World Population Data

We have scraped world population data from Wikipedia. Here are the details:

  • Country: The name of the country.
  • Population: The total population of the country.
  • % of World: The percentage of the world population represented by the country.
  • Date: The date when the population data was last updated.
  • Source: The source of the population data.

Top 20 Safest Countries

We have gathered information on the top 20 safest countries using the Global Peace Index:

  • Country Name: The name of the country.
  • Global Peace Index: A measure of the relative peacefulness of a country.

Top 10 Insurance Companies in Canada

We have compiled a list of the top 10 insurance companies in Canada:

  • Company: The name of the insurance company.
  • Merged Into: Information about any mergers involving the company.
  • Year: The year when the merger or other significant event occurred.
  • Note: Additional notes or details about the company.

We are using Flask module to create the website.
